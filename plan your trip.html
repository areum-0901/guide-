Plan Your Mongolian Adventure

Helpful tips and ideas for building your perfect itinerary

Step-by-Step Travel Planning

  1. Choose the best time: July and August are ideal for warm weather and festivals like Naadam. Winter offers snow and ice experiences.
  2. Pick your destinations: Ulaanbaatar, Gobi Desert, Lake Khövsgöl, Terelj, Orkhon Valley, and more.
  3. Decide your style: Budget backpacking, cultural tours, eco-lodging, or luxury gers?
  4. Book transportation: Domestic flights, vans, buses, or private drivers.
  5. Arrange accommodations: Hotels, hostels, guesthouses, or traditional ger camps.

Suggested Tour Itineraries

  • 3-Day Tour: Ulaanbaatar + Terelj National Park
  • 7-Day Tour: Gobi Desert + Karakorum
  • 10-Day Adventure: Gobi + Lake Khövsgöl + cultural homestay
  • 14+ Days: Explore central, northern, and western Mongolia in depth

Travel Essentials

  • Valid passport and a Mongolian visa (check entry rules for your country)
  • Travel insurance with medical coverage
  • Warm and layered clothing — even summers can be cool at night
  • Cash in local currency (Tögrög); card use is limited in rural areas

Tips for Travelers

  • Learn a few basic Mongolian phrases — locals appreciate it!
  • Pack snacks and a power bank for long journeys
  • Respect local customs and nomadic traditions
  • Always ask before taking photos of people
