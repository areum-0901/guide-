Discover Terelj National Park

A paradise of nature, adventure, and culture near Ulaanbaatar

About Terelj

Gorkhi-Terelj National Park is one of Mongolia’s most accessible and picturesque protected areas. Located just 55 km from Ulaanbaatar, it offers a unique blend of rugged landscapes, spiritual sites, and traditional nomadic culture.

Things to See and Do

  • Marvel at Turtle Rock (Melkhii Khad) and its natural shape
  • Climb up to Aryabal Meditation Temple for panoramic views
  • Ride horses across open valleys and forested hills
  • Meet local nomadic families and experience ger life
  • Hike through pristine trails or relax by the Terelj River

Wildlife and Nature

The park is home to diverse wildlife including red deer, marmots, and many bird species. In spring and summer, wildflowers blanket the hillsides, while autumn brings vivid golden hues.

How to Get There

Terelj is just a 1.5-hour drive from Ulaanbaatar. You can hire a taxi, book a tour, or take a local bus to the park entrance. Many guesthouses and camps offer pickup services as well.

Travel Tips

  • Pack warm clothes—even in summer, nights can be chilly
  • Bring cash for local souvenirs, food, or camel rides
  • Most camps have limited electricity—bring a power bank
  • Respect the natural environment and local customs
